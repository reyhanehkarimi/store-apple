What is Mac

The Mac, short for Macintosh (its official name until 1999),
is a family of personal computers designed and marketed by Apple Inc.
The product lineup includes the MacBook Air and MacBook Pro laptops,
and the iMac, Mac Mini, Mac Studio, and Mac Pro desktops.
Macs are sold with the macOS operating system.

What is a MacBook

The MacBook is a brand of Macintosh laptop computers designed by Apple.
These laptop computers make use of the macOS operating system since 2006.
In 2005, the Mac switched to Intel CPUs instead of PowerPC processors,
and the PowerBook and iBook names were replaced by MacBook.
